FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is SUM+IT?

SUM+IT is web-based software developed to rapidly speed up the process of quoting trusses and other building components supplied by most wood truss component manufacturers.

Is SUM+IT worth the investment?

SUM+IT offers discounts for annual subscriptions and multiple users.  Pricing is set in a way to give customers a return on their investment even if it is only used occasionally.

Will I need to design anything for a price?

SUM+IT skips any design stage and produces fast and accurate quotes.  

We get a lot of our work because of our design skills.  Won't my customers complain about not receving a layout with their price?

As good as your designers may be, there is only so much that they can do, and much of their time is spent working on quotes that never become orders.  Customers value excellent customer service ahead of providing more information at the quote stage. Sales are limited to quotes, and SUM+IT can multiply your quoted dollars many times over. More quotes = more sales.

Can my sales people use it?  My designers are too busy.

While you don't need to be proficient with truss design software to use SUM+IT, you will need to have a reasonable comprehension of wood frame construction.  Many sales people have the knowledge to use it, while others may not.  If your designers do need to use it, they will still save a lot of time.  You will have a more productive work flow process with SUM+IT.
